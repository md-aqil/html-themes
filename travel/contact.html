    Do I need to pay for opening my store?

    No, Bigly is a free of cost store/mobile app for you.

    What’s the difference between earning from Bigly Store and earning from Bigly App?

    Bigly Store is a website that allows you to create your free online store. You can share this store link on your Facebook page. Customers can click on the link to buy products and order there itself. Bigly App is a mobile app that allows you to open your account to choose products for re-selling. Here you can upload product photos on your Facebook page and take orders via WhatsApp, Facebook messenger or any other means. You take the order and make that order in the Bigly App for the wholesaler to deliver it to your customer.

    How much time will it take to set this up?

    Ans. It can be set up in two clicks which takes five minutes. But you can spend as much time as you want to personalize your store, choose your product categories and add your profit margin.

    How much can I earn with this?

    There’s no limit to this earning, it all depends on your followers or page visits on the Facebook page. Bigly does not restrict your income.

    Can I share store link on my other social media channels?

    Yes, you can share our store link on WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook or any other channel.

    Do I need to get the products delivered to customers?

    No, when your customer makes a purchase and adds his/her address, we deliver to that address.

    How many cities are you delivering in?

    Ans. We deliver to 20,000 pincodes across India.
